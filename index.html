What's Dodow?

Dodow is a metronome with a light system that helps you fall asleep naturally, without taking any medicine. Dodow started selling in France 2 years ago. So far, more than 70,000 people are falling asleep with Dodow :)

How's it work?

Focusing on the light beam projected on the ceiling slows down the flow of your thoughts. At the same time, synchronizing your abdominal breathing with the light's movement gradually reduces your breathing rate from 11 to 6 breaths per minute. A very slow breathing rate allows you to regulate the autonomic nervous system. The rhythm of the light also has a hypnotic effect, which helps you get rid of the churning thoughts that keep you awake. After 8 minutes (length of the exercise), sleep is not far off!

Who's it for?

Anyone with problems falling asleep (from 6 years of age) : churning thoughts, stress, exam anxiety, waking up in the middle of the night.
